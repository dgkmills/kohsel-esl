Lesson 20: The Clear Communication Challenge

I. Warm-up: Sequential Language Review

Remember the key phrases for explaining steps from Lesson 19?

  • Starting: First,..., To begin with,...
  • Continuing: Next,..., Then,..., After that,..., Once X is complete,...
  • Ending: Finally,..., The last step is...
  • Clarifying: The key point is..., It is critical that you..., Instead of X, you must do Y.

Activity ("Robot Teacher"): Think about a simple task (like making coffee or starting a machine). How would you instruct someone using these sequential phrases?

(Practice this mentally or with a colleague!)
